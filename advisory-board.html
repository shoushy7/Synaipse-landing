Clinical Advisory Board

Physician-Led. Purpose-Driven.

Our Clinical Advisory Board is composed of practicing physicians who understand the challenges of independent practice firsthand. They steer Synaipse's product development with a single goal: empowering independent physicians to thrive.

Our Approach

Technology Guided by Clinicians, Not Consultants

Most healthcare technology is built by people who have never treated a patient. Features are designed in boardrooms, not exam rooms. The result? Tools that add to the burden instead of relieving it.

Synaipse is different. Our Clinical Advisory Board puts practicing physicians at the helm of product development. Every workflow, every automation, every interface decision is evaluated by clinicians who will use it â€” and who understand what independent practices actually need.

The board meets regularly to review product direction, validate clinical workflows, and ensure every feature we ship genuinely reduces administrative burden for independent practices. If it doesn't serve that mission, it doesn't ship.

Independent Practice Advocates

Every board member champions the needs of independent practices, ensuring our tools protect physician autonomy.

Clinical Workflow Validation

No feature ships without physician sign-off. The board validates that every automation mirrors real clinical practice.

Burden Reduction Focus

The single metric: does this give physicians more time with patients? If not, it goes back to the drawing board.
