Operations & Leadership

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Operations·Cincinnati, OH·Full-time·Remote/Hybrid
Competitive Salary
Significant Founding Equity

About the Role

As our founding COO, you will build and scale the operational engine that powers Synaipse's growth. While our physician co-founders focus on product vision and clinical strategy, you will own go-to-market execution, business development, financial operations, and team building. You will report directly to the CEO.

This is a rare opportunity to shape a healthcare AI company from the ground up. You won't inherit processes — you'll create them. From closing the first enterprise contracts to building the sales playbook to managing vendor relationships, every operational decision is yours to make.

What You'll Own

  • Go-to-market strategy and execution — identify target customer segments, build the sales pipeline, and close deals with independent practices, surgical groups, and health systems
  • Strategic partnerships with EHR vendors, payer organizations, medical societies, and healthcare technology platforms
  • Financial planning and operations — budgeting, forecasting, fundraising support, and unit economics optimization
  • Operational infrastructure — establish the processes, tools, and workflows that allow a small team to execute at enterprise scale
  • Team building — recruit, hire, and develop the founding team across sales, customer success, and operations

What We're Looking For

  • 8+ years of experience in operations, strategy, or business development, with at least 3 years in a leadership role at a high-growth startup or healthcare company
  • Proven track record of taking a B2B SaaS or healthcare product to market — ideally from early-stage through initial revenue milestones
  • Deep understanding of the healthcare industry landscape: payer dynamics, practice operations, credentialing workflows, or revenue cycle management
  • Experience building and leading cross-functional teams in a fast-paced, resource-constrained environment
  • Strong financial acumen — comfortable with P&L ownership, fundraising, and investor relations
  • Bias toward action — you'd rather ship an 80% solution today than a perfect one next quarter

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive base salary commensurate with experience
  • Significant founding equity stake — own a meaningful piece of what we're building
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Remote-first with optional hybrid at 1819 Innovation Hub, Cincinnati
  • Direct partnership with physician co-founders who understand the clinical problems firsthand

Engineering

Founding Senior Frontend Engineer

Engineering·Cincinnati, OH·Full-time·Hybrid
Significant Equity

About the Role

Your mission is to build the Command Center — the high-velocity React interface that powers every daily workflow at Synaipse. You'll create the unified workspace where front desk staff manage intake, clinicians review AI-generated notes, and billing teams handle the entire revenue cycle — all without leaving a single browser tab.

This is the interface that replaces toggling between 4-6 applications per patient case. Every pixel, every interaction, every millisecond of latency matters. You'll work directly with physician end-users to ship features that eliminate hours of administrative work daily.

What You'll Build

  • Role-based workspaces — purpose-built views for front desk (scheduling, eligibility, referrals), clinical staff (ambient scribing, note review, orders), and billing (coding, PA, claims, denials)
  • A real-time dashboard consolidating patient journey status, authorization tracking, and revenue cycle KPIs in one view
  • High-performance document handling — PDF rendering, fax image processing, and inline annotation for clinical documentation review
  • AI-powered UI components: confidence-scored coding suggestions, auto-populated form fields from referral data, and smart search across patient records
  • Accessible, responsive design that works seamlessly across desktop and tablet for clinical environments

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years of frontend engineering experience with React and TypeScript
  • Proven track record building complex, data-dense interfaces — think dashboards, workflow tools, or enterprise SaaS
  • Deep understanding of state management (Redux, Zustand, or similar), performance optimization, and component architecture
  • Experience with Canvas API, PDF.js, or similar document rendering technologies is a strong plus
  • Eye for design and UX — ability to translate requirements into clean, intuitive interfaces without detailed mockups
  • Comfort working in a fast-paced startup where you'll ship features measured in days, not sprints

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive base salary commensurate with experience
  • Significant founding equity stake — own what you build
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Hybrid work from 1819 Innovation Hub, Cincinnati with flexible remote days
  • Direct access to physician co-founders and real clinical users for rapid feedback loops
