AI Document Intelligence

Upload any credential document — license, DEA certificate, board certification, malpractice policy, CV — and our AI extracts every field automatically. No more manual data entry from scanned PDFs.

  • Claude Vision extracts structured data from PDFs and images
  • Automatic field mapping to provider profiles
  • Supports licenses, DEA, board certs, education, training, and malpractice records
  • Confidence scoring with human-in-the-loop review for edge cases

Provider Credential Vault

A single, secure source of truth for every provider's credentials. Track completeness, spot gaps, and know exactly what's on file — with real-time expiration alerts so nothing lapses.

  • Centralized storage for all provider credentials and documents
  • Completeness tracking with gap identification
  • Automatic expiration monitoring and renewal alerts
  • Secure document vault with role-based access control

Application Lifecycle Management

Track every credentialing application from draft through submission, review, and final approval. Requirements checklists, status transitions, and a full audit trail — built for hospital and payer enrollment workflows.

  • Full lifecycle tracking: draft → submitted → under review → approved
  • Per-application requirements checklists with completion tracking
  • Automated packet assembly — PDF and ZIP with cover sheet and table of contents
  • Hospital and payer portal credential management

Autonomous Follow-Up Engine

Stop chasing hospitals and payers for status updates. Our AI agent drafts and sends follow-up communications on a configurable cadence, escalating to your admin team only when human judgment is needed.

  • AI-drafted follow-up emails with configurable send cadence
  • Automatic escalation after configurable attempt thresholds
  • Multi-channel tracking: email, phone, fax, and portal submissions
  • Daily digest emails with pending actions and application status summaries

Expiration Monitoring Dashboard

Never let a license, DEA, or board certification lapse again. The expiration dashboard runs daily sweeps across every provider and every credential type, surfacing what needs attention before it becomes a problem.

  • Daily automated sweeps across all providers and credential types
  • Configurable alert windows (30, 60, 90 days before expiration)
  • Dashboard view with at-a-glance status for your entire practice
  • Automatic renewal reminders sent to providers and admin staff

CAQH ProView, NPI Registry & NPDB

Sync provider data directly from CAQH ProView, the CMS NPPES registry, and the National Practitioner Data Bank. Keep profiles current and compliance verified without re-entering data that already exists in national databases.

  • CAQH ProView data sync with attestation status tracking
  • Direct NPI registry lookup from CMS NPPES
  • NPDB query integration for adverse action and malpractice history
  • Automated profile enrichment from external data sources
How It Works

From 21 Manual Steps to Autonomous

Traditional credentialing requires an admin to manually perform every step. Synaipse Credentialing replaces the entire process with an intelligent pipeline.

1

Upload & Extract

Drop in provider documents. AI extracts every field and builds the provider profile automatically.

2

Assemble & Submit

Application packets are assembled with cover sheets and submitted to hospitals and payers electronically.

3

Track & Follow Up

The system tracks every application, sends follow-ups on schedule, and escalates only when it needs you.

4

Monitor & Renew

Credentials are monitored continuously. You get alerts before anything expires, with renewal workflows ready to go.
