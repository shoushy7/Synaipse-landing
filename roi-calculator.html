How We Calculate Your Savings

Intake Agent Savings

The Intake Agent automates patient registration, insurance verification, and eligibility checks. We estimate each provider saves 12 hours per month on intake tasks, with each hour of staff time valued at $45. The calculation assumes 8 tasks per hour that can be automated.

Formula: Providers × 12 hours/month × 8 tasks/hour × $45/hour × 12 months

Prior Auth Agent Savings

The Prior Authorization Agent handles submission, tracking, and follow-up of prior authorizations. We estimate 30% of patients require a prior auth, each taking 45 minutes of staff time at $45/hour. Synaipse automates 70% of these workflows.

Formula: Monthly patients × 30% × 45 min × ($45/60) × 70% automation rate × 12 months

Billing Agent Savings

The Billing Agent reduces coding errors, accelerates claim submission, and improves clean claim rates. We estimate Synaipse recovers 2% of total revenue that would otherwise be lost to billing errors, under-coding, and delayed submissions.

Formula: Monthly patients × Avg revenue per patient × 2% recovery rate × 12 months

Denial Agent Savings

The Denial Agent automatically identifies, categorizes, and appeals denied claims. Based on industry benchmarks, Synaipse recovers 60% of denied revenue that would otherwise go unworked or be written off.

Formula: Monthly patients × Avg revenue per patient × Denial rate × 60% recovery × 12 months
