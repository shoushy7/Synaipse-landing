Trust & Security

Your Data Security Is Our Top Priority

Synaipse is built on a foundation of HIPAA compliance, SOC 2 Type II certification, and physician-led security governance. We treat your patients' data with the same care you give your patients.

HIPAA Compliant

Full compliance with HIPAA Privacy, Security, and Breach Notification Rules

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited security controls for availability, confidentiality, and integrity

BAA Available

Business Associate Agreements available for all customers prior to PHI access

How We Protect Your Data

End-to-End Encryption

All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Your patients' information is never exposed in plain text at any point in the pipeline.

Access Controls

Role-based access control (RBAC), mandatory multi-factor authentication, and comprehensive audit logging ensure only authorized personnel access sensitive data.

Infrastructure

Hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure with US-based data centers. No patient data ever leaves the United States.

Business Associate Agreement

We execute a BAA with every customer before any PHI is processed. Our BAA covers all Synaipse products and services by default.

Incident Response

Our documented incident response plan includes 24-hour breach notification to affected parties and regulators, exceeding HIPAA requirements.

Penetration Testing

Regular third-party penetration testing and security assessments validate our defenses. Findings are remediated promptly and verified in follow-up audits.

HIPAA Compliance in Detail

Synaipse maintains full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), including the Privacy Rule, Security Rule, and Breach Notification Rule. Our compliance program is overseen by our designated Privacy and Security Officers and reviewed annually.

Administrative Safeguards
  • Security Management Process: Regular risk assessments identify and mitigate potential threats to ePHI.
  • Workforce Security: Background checks, role-based access provisioning, and timely termination procedures for departing employees.
  • Information Access Management: Minimum necessary access principle enforced across all systems handling PHI.
  • Security Awareness Training: All employees complete HIPAA security training at onboarding and annually thereafter.
  • Contingency Planning: Documented disaster recovery and emergency mode operation plans with regular testing.
Physical Safeguards
  • Facility Access Controls: Cloud infrastructure hosted in SOC 2 certified data centers with biometric access, 24/7 surveillance, and multi-layer physical security.
  • Workstation Security: All employee workstations use full-disk encryption, automatic screen locks, and endpoint detection and response (EDR) software.
  • Device and Media Controls: Strict policies for media disposal and reuse, including cryptographic erasure for decommissioned storage devices.
Technical Safeguards
  • Access Control: Unique user identification, automatic session timeout, and mandatory multi-factor authentication for all systems.
  • Audit Controls: Comprehensive logging of all access to ePHI with automated alerting for anomalous activity.
  • Integrity Controls: Mechanisms to authenticate ePHI and detect unauthorized alteration or destruction.
  • Transmission Security: TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit, with certificate pinning for critical API communications.
  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for all data at rest, with customer-managed encryption keys available for enterprise plans.

How We Handle Your Data

Data Collection

Synaipse collects only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. This includes practice information, provider credentials, patient demographic and insurance data required for claims processing, and usage analytics. We never collect data beyond what is needed for the specific service you use.

Data Storage

All data is stored in encrypted databases within US-based data centers operated by SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers. Data is logically isolated between customers, and access is restricted through role-based access controls and network segmentation.

Data Retention

We retain customer data for the duration of the service agreement plus the period required by applicable regulations (typically 6 years for HIPAA-covered records). Retention periods are clearly defined in our BAA and can be customized per customer requirements.

Data Deletion

Upon contract termination or customer request, we permanently delete all customer data within 30 days using cryptographic erasure methods. A certificate of destruction is provided upon request. Backup copies are purged within 90 days following the same process.

Third Parties

Synaipse carefully vets all subprocessors and requires each to execute a BAA and meet our security standards before accessing any PHI. Our current subprocessor list is available upon request, and customers are notified 30 days before any new subprocessor is added.

Privacy Policy

Synaipse is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers, their staff, and their patients. Our privacy practices are designed to comply with HIPAA, state privacy laws, and emerging healthcare data protection standards.

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary to deliver our automation services.
  • Patient health information (PHI) is never sold, shared for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose beyond the contracted services.
  • We do not use PHI to train machine learning models without explicit, written customer consent.
  • All employees with access to PHI are subject to confidentiality agreements and regular HIPAA training.
  • Customers retain full ownership of their data at all times. Synaipse acts solely as a Business Associate under HIPAA.
  • We maintain a transparent data processing register and provide customers with detailed information about how their data is processed upon request.
  • Individual patients may request access to, correction of, or restrictions on their PHI through their healthcare provider, who can coordinate with Synaipse as needed.
  • Our privacy practices are reviewed and updated at least annually. Material changes are communicated to all active customers 30 days in advance.

For the full Privacy Policy document, or to submit a privacy-related request, please contact privacy@synaipse.org.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Synaipse HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Synaipse is fully HIPAA compliant. We implement all required administrative, physical, and technical safeguards as mandated by the HIPAA Security Rule. Our compliance program is validated through independent third-party audits, and we maintain comprehensive documentation of our policies and procedures.

Do you sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)?

Absolutely. We execute a Business Associate Agreement with every customer before any PHI is accessed or processed. Our standard BAA is available for review during the sales process, and we accommodate reasonable modifications to meet your organization's requirements.

Where is my data stored?

All customer data is stored in US-based data centers operated by SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure providers. Data never leaves the United States. Our infrastructure is deployed across multiple availability zones for redundancy and high availability.

How is my data encrypted?

Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256, the same standard used by the US government for classified information. Data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3 encryption. Encryption keys are managed through a dedicated key management service with automatic rotation and strict access controls.

What happens in the event of a data breach?

Our incident response plan is activated immediately upon detection of any potential breach. Affected customers are notified within 24 hours -- exceeding the HIPAA requirement of 60 days. Our response team investigates the scope, contains the incident, and provides a detailed post-incident report including remediation steps taken to prevent recurrence.

Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days of the request using cryptographic erasure methods. Backup copies are purged within 90 days. A certificate of destruction is provided upon request.

Does Synaipse sell my data?

Never. Synaipse does not sell, share, or monetize customer data or patient health information in any way. Your data is used exclusively to provide the services you have contracted for. We do not use PHI for marketing, analytics, or AI model training without explicit written consent.

Who are your subprocessors?

We maintain a current list of subprocessors that have access to customer data. All subprocessors are required to execute BAAs and meet our security standards. We notify customers at least 30 days before engaging any new subprocessor. The full subprocessor list is available upon request -- please contact security@synaipse.org.

Security Questions?

Have Security Questions?

Our team is ready to discuss your security and compliance requirements. We provide detailed documentation, security questionnaire responses, and BAAs to every prospective customer.
